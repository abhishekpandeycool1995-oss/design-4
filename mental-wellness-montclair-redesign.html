About Our Practice

A Whole-Person Approach to Mental Wellness

At Mental Wellness Montclair, we believe in looking at the whole person as we help you meet your goals. We are a dedicated therapy office committed to offering support to individuals, couples, and families within our community.

Our mission is to create a nurturing environment where clients can freely express themselves and foster personal development. We take a whole-person approach to care, focusing on your goals, experiences, and emotional well-being in a welcoming environment designed for open conversation and meaningful progress.

With office hours of Monday–Friday 8AM–8PM and Saturdays 8AM–4PM, we are here to assist you when you need us most.
